# Библия персонажей и локаций — «Манас»

Правило: английские блоки копируются в промт **слово в слово** при каждом появлении персонажа/локации. Не переформулировать.

## Константы проекта (зафиксировано)

**Орнамент (единая формулировка):**
```
chevron-and-diamond ilme-oymo geometric pattern
```

**Порода/типаж коней (единая формулировка):**
```
compact powerful Central Asian steppe horse, thick arched neck, sturdy short legs, long thick mane and tail
```

**Формат кадра:** `16:9 cinematic aspect ratio` — подтверждён пользователем для всех серий, включая рилс.

## Персонажи

### МАНАС — младенец (серия 1)
```
Newborn Kyrgyz baby boy, unusually large and strong, thick black hair,
fierce dark eyes already open and alert, right fist clenched tight,
swaddled in dark felt wraps with a thin red cord
```

### МАНАС — мальчик 12 лет (серия 2)
```
Kyrgyz boy of twelve, unusually tall and broad-shouldered for his age,
thick unruly black hair, fierce dark eyes under heavy straight brows,
calm unsettling confidence in his face, wearing a plain ochre shepherd's
tunic belted with a narrow red sash, rough sheepskin vest, worn leather
boots
```
*Красный кушак = маркер «свой» по правилу 4.3, присутствует всегда.*

### ЖАКЫП — отец
```
Kyrgyz man in his early sixties, weathered deeply lined face, thin grey
beard, heavy tired eyes carrying decades of grief, wearing a dark brown
fur-trimmed chapan robe with chevron-and-diamond ilme-oymo geometric
pattern trim over a faded ochre tunic, tall white felt kalpak hat with
dark brim, broad leather belt
```

### ЧЫЙЫРДЫ — мать
```
Kyrgyz woman in her early fifties, dignified lined face, dark steady
eyes of quiet resolve, wearing a white elechek layered turban headdress
and a deep burnt-red velvet chapan over a dark dress, heavy silver
earrings
```

### АКБАЛТА — старейшина
```
Kyrgyz elder in his late sixties, broad weathered face, full white
beard, sharp watchful eyes, wearing a charcoal-grey quilted chapan with
chevron-and-diamond ilme-oymo geometric pattern trim, tall white felt
kalpak, gnarled hands resting on a carved wooden staff
```

### ДЕРВИШ (Айкожо) — вестник судьбы
```
Ancient wandering dervish, long white beard reaching his chest,
deep-set luminous pale eyes, dusty white robes worn to grey at the
hem, tall pointed white felt cap, gnarled wooden staff, an air of
otherworldly stillness
```

### ЭСЕНХАН — антагонист
```
Enemy khan in his fifties, gaunt sharp-boned face, thin drooping
grey-black moustache, cold narrow eyes, ornate dark lacquered lamellar
armor, black fur-collared cloak, small pale grey-white banner hanging
behind his throne
```
*Бледный серо-белый акцент = маркер «чужой» по правилу 4.3.*

### ОШПУР — пастух-наставник
```
Old Kyrgyz shepherd in his sixties, sun-blackened wiry frame, sparse
grey beard, squinting kind eyes, patched sheepskin coat, shapeless felt
cap, long wooden herding staff
```

### КАЛМЫЦКИЕ ВСАДНИКИ — массовка врага
```
Enemy horsemen in dark leather-and-iron lamellar armor and fur-lined
helmets, every one of them carrying only small pale grey-white pennants
on their spears — no other banner colors anywhere among them
```
*Явное ограничение цвета массовки — фикс проблемы из 4.14 (случайные синие/красные флаги на фоне).*

### АККУЛА — конь Манаса
Жеребёнок (серия 1):
```
Newborn pale grey-white foal, wet and unsteady on long legs, dark
intelligent eyes, standing beside its grey mare mother
```
Молодой конь (серия 2):
```
Young pale grey-white stallion, compact powerful Central Asian steppe
horse, thick arched neck, sturdy short legs, long thick smoke-grey mane
and tail, dark intelligent eyes
```
