# Серия 1 «Пророчество» — видео-промты (image-to-video)

Как использовать: сгенерированный кадр из ep01-prophecy.md подаётся как первый кадр (image-to-video), промт ниже описывает движение. Длительность 5–10 сек на кадр.

**Общее правило крови/жёсткости:** любые удары и кровь — только через силуэт, вспышку пыли, контражур и скоростное размытие; никогда в деталях. Мощь держится ритмом движения камеры и среды, не натурализмом.

---

### КАДР 1 — Рассвет над стойбищем

VIDEO PROMPT (EN):
```
Slow forward aerial drift over the sleeping ail, 8 seconds. The thin band of dawn light at the horizon gradually brightens and warms, low mist rolls between the yurts in slow currents, thin smoke columns rise and lean gently in the wind, horses in the herd shift and lower their heads. Camera glides forward and very slightly downward, cinematic crane feel, no tilt wobble. Atmosphere layers move at different parallax speeds: near mist faster, far mountains static. The living-painting rule: preserve the matte oil-painting texture and visible canvas grain in every frame, brushstrokes subtly shimmering as things move — the image must move like a living painting, never resolving into photorealism. No morphing, no warping geometry, no flicker, no added text or watermarks.
```

### КАДР 2 — Жакып на холме

VIDEO PROMPT (EN):
```
Slow dolly-in from behind toward the lone figure's shoulder, 8 seconds, rising almost imperceptibly to just above his shoulder height. His fur-trimmed chapan and the dry grass ripple in a steady wind, distant horse herds drift slowly across the valley below, heavy clouds slide overhead changing the diffused light in one slow soft wave across the land. He never moves — his stillness against a moving world is the shot. Depth layers in parallax: foreground grass fastest, herds slower, mountains static. Preserve the matte oil-painting texture and visible canvas grain in every frame, brushstrokes subtly shimmering — a living painting, never photorealism. No morphing, no warping, no flicker.
```

### КАДР 14 — Рождение Аккулы (финал)

```
Low tracking shot sliding slowly sideways at foal height, 10 seconds. The newborn pale foal gathers its long legs and takes its first unsteady step — wobbles — catches itself — and stands firm, head rising. The grey mare turns her head toward it, breath steaming. Ground mist parts around the foal's legs as it moves. Behind them the dawn band at the horizon visibly brightens through the shot, the warm glow strengthening. In the final second the foal lifts its head into the light and holds still — a natural freeze-frame for the title card. Preserve the matte oil-painting texture and visible canvas grain in every frame — a living painting, never photorealism. Dawn warmth stays concentrated at the horizon. No morphing, no warping legs, no flicker.
```
